# Vision & Mission

## Vision

Build the world's most trusted mobile-first peer-to-peer cross-border shipping platform.

Karri begins with the East African diaspora, then expands corridor by corridor into a global trust network.

## Mission

Help senders and travelers move packages safely through verified identity, transparent custody, reliable communication, and community reputation.

## Why Karri exists

Cross-border shipping is often expensive, slow, opaque, or informal.

Karri exists to make peer-to-peer shipping feel organized, safe, and trackable.

## The promise

Every sender should know:

- Who has the package
- Where the package is in the journey
- What happens next
- What proof exists
- Who is accountable
