# Brand Guidelines

## Brand idea

Karri helps community shipping feel clearer, calmer, and more accountable. The visual identity should feel trustworthy and modern without borrowing the visual language of banks, border agencies, or luxury logistics brands.

The brand is rooted in East African diaspora connection, but it should not reduce that community to flags, patterns, or decorative clichés. Product language and real corridor needs carry the cultural context more honestly than ornamental motifs.

## Brand qualities

- **Trustworthy:** clear hierarchy, visible limitations, and consistent status language.
- **Calm:** cream backgrounds, generous spacing, and restrained color.
- **Premium:** careful typography and surfaces, not gloss or exclusivity.
- **Community-centered:** warm copy and mutual responsibility rather than transactional urgency.
- **Mobile-first:** readable in one hand, with large touch targets and short decisions.

## Core palette

| Role | Token | Value | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Trust green | `primary` | `#276749` | Primary actions, active states, route emphasis |
| Deep forest | `text` | `#172A21` | Main text and high-emphasis headings |
| Soft cream | `background` | `#F8F6F0` | App canvas |
| Sky blue | `sky` | `#347EB8` | Information and Development Mode |
| Warm gold | `gold` | `#B8872D` | Warm contextual accents and trust-centered education |
| Success | `success` | `#2F7D57` | Confirmed successful UI outcomes |
| Warning | `warning` | `#AD681C` | Caution that needs attention |
| Error | `error` | `#B54740` | Failed actions and invalid state |

Soft variants support banners, badges, and chips. Neutral surfaces and borders carry most of the interface so semantic colors remain meaningful.

## Color rules

- Trust green means action or active state; it does not mean a person is verified.
- Gold adds warmth. It must not imply payment, premium access, or guaranteed trust.
- Sky blue is the default informational tone and the only tone for Development Mode notices.
- Success appears only after an operation succeeds.
- Never rely on color alone: pair color with text, a status dot, or a clear label.

## Voice

Use plain, steady language. Explain what Karri knows, what it does not know, and what happens next.

Prefer:

- “Possible match” over “Perfect match.”
- “Exact corridor” over “Verified route.”
- “Start a Karri session” over technical Firebase language in primary copy.
- “Nothing to track yet” over “No data.”

Avoid urgency, guarantees, unexplained scoring, and claims that identity, custody, or safety has been verified before the supporting workflow exists.

## Logo

Use the current Karri mark on a quiet background with its proportions intact. Keep at least one small spacing token around it, do not tint the raster asset, and do not use it as a generic status icon.
