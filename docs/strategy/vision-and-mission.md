# Vision and Mission

## Vision

Build the world's most trusted mobile-first peer-to-peer cross-border shipping network, beginning with the East African diaspora and expanding globally one corridor at a time.

## Mission

Help senders and travelers coordinate cross-border package delivery with clear expectations, verified participation, visible custody, and dependable communication.

## The problem we choose to solve

Informal cross-border handoffs often work because communities have strong relationships, but they become fragile when the sender and traveler do not know one another. Important questions remain unanswered: Is the route compatible? What is in the package? What reward was offered? Who currently has it? Was delivery confirmed?

Karri turns those questions into explicit product states. It does not claim to remove every risk. It gives participants a consistent way to understand the agreement, record responsibility, and make informed choices.

## What trust means at Karri

Trust is evidence presented in context:

- An authenticated account and a complete profile.
- A clear shipment or trip listing.
- A recorded booking accepted by both parties.
- An append-only sequence of custody events.
- Reviews tied to completed bookings.
- A trust score whose inputs and limitations can be explained.

## Expansion principle

Karri will not treat global expansion as a translation exercise. Every new corridor needs validated demand, local prohibited-item guidance, handoff expectations, support readiness, and appropriate legal review. Remote configuration and feature flags allow the platform to open capability gradually without maintaining different apps.
